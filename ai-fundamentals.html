A bit of history

Antiquity

Enlightenment

  • Descarte says animals are just complex machines.
  • Hobbes says that "...reason is nothing but reckoning".
  • Julien Offray de La Mettrie published the book Man a Machine

20th Century

  • John McCarthy coins the term Artificial Intelligence and invents LISP in the 50's.
  • Perceptrons lay the foundation for modern deep learning
  • Rules-based AI fails leading to the AI Winter
  • Geoffery Hinton figures out modern Neural Networks, leading to the state of things today
  • Deep Blue beats Kasparov using brute force proving that computational resources were abundant enough to outperform humans in complex tasks
